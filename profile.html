Company Overview

BG Nubong & Associates is a professional accounting and advisory firm offering reliable, accurate, and compliant financial solutions to a diverse client base. Our approach combines technical expertise, ethical practice, and a deep understanding of business environments to support informed decision-making and sustainable growth.

Legal Status

The firm operates as a Proprietary Limited (Pty Ltd) company registered in South Africa and conducts its activities in full compliance with applicable statutory and regulatory requirements.

Professional Focus

Our services are centered on accounting, auditing, taxation, and business advisory. We provide practical and compliant financial solutions designed to meet the operational and regulatory needs of modern businesses.

Industries Served

We work with small and medium-sized enterprises, non-profit organizations, startups, and established corporates across various sectors, offering tailored services aligned with each client’s objectives.

Professional Standards

All engagements are performed in accordance with International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS) and IFRS for SMEs. We are committed to accuracy, transparency, and consistent application of professional standards.

Ethical Commitment

Integrity, confidentiality, and independence are fundamental to our practice. We uphold strict ethical principles to protect client interests and maintain professional credibility in every engagement.

Managing Director

Barbara Nubong CA(SA) RA

Position: Managing Director & Principal Accountant

Qualifications: Chartered Accountant (SA), Registered Auditor

Barbara Nubong is a qualified Chartered Accountant and Registered Auditor, registered with SAICA and IRBA respectively. She obtained a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration from Oklahoma State University, USA, and later a Postgraduate Diploma in Applied Accounting Sciences from the University of South Africa (UNISA). Barbara qualified as a CA(SA) in 2014 and became a Registered Auditor in 2016, founding BG Nubong and Associates Inc., a black-owned audit firm performing audits across both public and private sectors in South Africa.

As Managing Director, Barbara has directed and gained extensive experience with clients across Property and Construction, Municipal accounts, Labour-related services, NGOs, Government departments, Telecommunications, Public schools, and the Hospitality industry.

She possesses mastery in auditing and accounting principles including International Auditing Standards (IAS), International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS), Generally Recognized Accounting Practice (GRAP), US GAAP, PFMA, and National Treasury Regulations.

Barbara has also independently consulted for Morar Incorporated performing Internal Auditing reviews for organizations including:

  • Department of Public Enterprises (DPE) – Financial Management Review
  • Construction Industry Development Board (CIDB) – Stakeholder Management Review
  • Financial and Fiscal Commission (FFC) – Stakeholder Management Review
  • National Health Laboratory Services (NHLS) – Contract Management Review
  • Ondesterpoort Biological Products SOC Ltd – Review on Production and Marketing processes

BG Nubong and Associates has performed audits and reviews for large enterprises such as Iketsetse Enterprise Network NPO (CWP contract), Deloitte Consulting Overseas Projects LLC (USAID Southern Energy Programme), and the Civilian Secretariat for Police Service (SAPS). Barbara successfully managed these audits, ensuring client satisfaction and excellent service delivery.
