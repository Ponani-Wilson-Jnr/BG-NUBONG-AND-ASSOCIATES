Company Overview

BG Nubong and Associates Inc (CIPC No: 2016/286037/21) is a black-owned professional firm of Chartered Accountants (SA) and Registered Auditors, established in 2016. The firm provides accounting, auditing, taxation, and advisory services to clients across a wide range of industries in South Africa.

We are committed to delivering high-quality professional services grounded in integrity, technical competence, and strict compliance with applicable accounting, auditing, and regulatory standards. Our work is guided by professionalism, independence, and a strong sense of responsibility to our clients and stakeholders.

Who We Are

We are a professional services firm specialising in Accounting, Audit and Assurance, Taxation, and Advisory services. Our team consists of qualified professionals who apply both South African and international standards to deliver accurate, practical, and reliable financial solutions.

Through our membership with the South African Institute of Chartered Accountants (SAICA) and registration with the Independent Regulatory Board for Auditors (IRBA), we maintain the highest professional standards and remain aligned with current developments in the accounting and auditing profession.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver professional accounting, auditing, and financial services of the highest quality, enabling our clients to make informed business decisions and achieve sustainable growth. We are committed to ethical conduct, accuracy, and full regulatory compliance in all our professional engagements.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a trusted and respected professional services firm, recognised for excellence in audit quality, sound professional judgment, and the consistent delivery of long-term value to our clients and stakeholders.

Our Core Values

Our firm operates on a foundation of integrity, professionalism, and accountability. We uphold strict confidentiality, maintain independence in all engagements, and approach our work with diligence and respect. Client-focused service is central to our practice, ensuring that every engagement is tailored to meet the specific needs of each client.

Why Choose Us

Since our establishment in 2016, we have developed experience across a broad range of industries, allowing us to provide practical and relevant financial solutions. Our attention to detail, strong regulatory knowledge, and personalised approach enable us to build long-term client relationships based on trust, reliability, and consistent service quality.

Professional & Regulatory Standing

BG Nubong and Associates Inc is registered with the South African Institute of Chartered Accountants (SAICA) and the Independent Regulatory Board for Auditors (IRBA). These professional registrations ensure that all services are delivered in accordance with the highest ethical, technical, and regulatory standards.
